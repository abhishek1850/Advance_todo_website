## 1. THE ATTACKER’S PHILOSOPHY
Attackers Arena is not a "to-do list"—it is a **Battleground for Discipline**. 
- **The Core Rule:** Consistency over everything.
- **The XP System:** Your effort is quantified. Every task completed builds your "Level" of discipline.
- **The AI Edge:** You have access to a high-performance strategist (Coach Aries) to ensure you never walk into a day without a plan.

---

## 2. THE COMMAND CENTER (DASHBOARD)
The Dashboard is where you start every "Attack." It provides a 30,000-foot view of your progress.

### 🛡️ Real-Time Intelligence
- **Weather-Based Greeting:** The site greets you with a "Smart Greeting" that changes based on the time of day and artificial weather conditions (Sun, Rain, Clouds) to keep the environment alive.
- **Daily Motivation:** A curated quote from historical legends (Swami Vivekananda, Ratan Tata, Chanakya, etc.) is presented every day to set your mindset.
- **Vital Stats:**
    - **Tasks Today:** Shows how many missions you've cleared out of your total list.
    - **Current Streak:** Tracks how many consecutive days you've been active.
    - **Productivity Score:** A dynamic 0-100% score that calculates your consistency and completion rates.

## 3. THE MISSION ENGINE (TASK MANAGEMENT)
Missions (Tasks) are the heart of the Arena. They are organized into three fronts:

### 📅 The Three Fronts (Horizons)
1.  **Daily Front (Tactical):** What you MUST win today. 
2.  **Monthly Front (Strategic):** Bigger goals that require sustained effort throughout the month.
3.  **Yearly Front (Visionary):** Life-defining goals. 
*Note: Yearly missions grant **2x XP**, recognizing the massive discipline required for long-term goals.*

### 🏷️ Drafting a Mission
When you create a task, you designate its details:
- **Priority Classes:**
    - **Critical (50 XP):** Must be done at all costs.
    - **High (35 XP):** High impact on your growth.
    - **Medium (20 XP):** Standard missions.
    - **Low (10 XP):** Small wins.
- **Energy Requirements:** Match the task to your current state (**Low, Medium, or High Energy**). Don't try to "Attack" a High-Energy critical task when you're drained—save it for your peak performance window.
- **Subtasks:** Break "Mission Impossible" into smaller "Win-of-the-Moment" steps.

### 🔄 Smart Rollover (Yesterday's Pending)
If you miss a daily task, it doesn't disappear. It appears in the **"Yesterday's Pending"** section on your dashboard. This creates a "debt" that you must settle or reschedule immediately to keep your Arena clean.

## 4. COACH ARIES (AI INTEGRATION)
Coach Aries is your personal productivity strategist, powered by **Google Gemini AI**. He is integrated into every part of the site.

### 🤖 How to use Coach Aries:
- **Plan My Day:** Click the "Plan My Day" button on the dashboard, and Aries will review your mission list and suggest an optimal order of attack.
- **Break It Down:** If a task has been stuck for days, the **Procrastination Alert** will appear. Click "Break It Down," and Aries will automatically split that complex task into easy subtasks.
- **Strategy Chat:** Use the Assistant View to ask Aries for advice on time management, beating laziness, or setting better goals.

## 5. REWARDS OF DISCIPLINE (GAMIFICATION)
The Arena is designed to trigger "Dopamine Rewards" for every bit of effort you put in.

### 🆙 Leveling Up
- Every task grants **XP**. When you fill your XP bar, you **Level Up**.
- Each level requires 15% more XP than the last, challenging you to become more efficient over time.
- Level milestones trigger **Audio-Visual Celebrations** (Confetti and Level-Up Sounds).

### 🔥 Streak Milestones
The site tracks your daily activity. Missing even one day breaks your streak.
- **3-Day Streak:** 🌱 Sprout Milestone
- **7-Day Streak:** 🔥 Week Warrior
- **21-Day Streak:** 🏆 Habit Formed (Based on habit-forming science).
- **100+ Days:** 🦁 legendary Status.

### 🏆 Badge System
Earn permanent badges for special feats:
- **Early Bird:** Clearing 5 tasks before noon.
- **Full Spectrum:** Having active missions in all 3 Horizons at once.
- **Century Club:** 100 Missions cleared.

## 6. THE FOCUS CHAMBER (DEEP WORK)
When it's time to work, the **Focus View** is your sanctuary.

- **Pomodoro Timer:** Preset durations (25, 45, or 60 minutes) to help you work in sprints.
- **Focus Mode:** Hides the sidebar, charts, and distractions. It shows only your **Current Focus Mission** and a timer.
- **Reward:** Completing a task while in Focus Mode provides a sense of accomplishment and clean data for your analytics.

---

## 7. THE MISSION LOG (JOURNALING)
True discipline requires reflection. The **Journal View** is where you review your performance.

### 📝 Daily Reflection Fields:
- **Wins Today:** What did you conquer?
- **Learnings:** What new knowledge did you gain?
- **Mistakes:** Be honest about your failures. This is how you avoid them next time.
- **Tomorrow's Intent:** State the one thing that MUST happen tomorrow to make it a success.
- **XP Bonus:** Logging your journal entry grants a mandatory **+25 XP Daily Bonus**.

## 8. INTELLIGENCE REPORTS (ANALYTICS)
Knowledge is power. The **Analytics View** tracks your history.

- **Weekly Activity Chart:** See your completion trends across the week.
- **Productivity Score Trend:** Watch your score rise as you build consistency.
- **Category Heatmap:** Find out where you spend most of your energy (Work vs. Personal vs. Health).
- **History View:** A permanent record of every mission you have ever completed in the Arena.
